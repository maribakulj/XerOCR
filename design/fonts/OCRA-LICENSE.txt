File: OCRA.pfa  (PostScript Type 1, ASCII; FontName "OCRA", version 001.000)

PROVENANCE
----------
The font's embedded notice reads:
    "Created by Sauter ... with FontForge (http://fontforge.sf.net)"
This matches John Sauter's "OCR-A font" project:
    https://sourceforge.net/projects/ocr-a-font/
described as "A free OCR-A font, conformant to ANSI X3.17-1977, in TrueType
format, with sources", released into the **PUBLIC DOMAIN**.

BUILD NOTE
----------
.pfa (Type 1) is not a web font format. Convert to woff2/otf for @font-face
(e.g. with fontforge or fonttools). Public-domain status permits this freely.
