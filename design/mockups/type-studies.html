Fluxisch Else by Ludivine Loiseau, Pierre Huyghebaert & OSP team. Drawn from scans of cheap 1960s–70s phototypesetting — the bumps and curves are literally optical-bench artefacts. Exactly the print-to-digital lineage you mentioned. Body in Plex Sans for readability.
